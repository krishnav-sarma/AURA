ABOUT
AURA is a student-led group designed to enhance the college experience by offering unlimited benefits to its members. It provides academic support, project help, and most important financial helps i.e (if you have money and wanted to spend, we can help) NOTE:borrowing small amount money is never given back . The group also offers physical and emotional support, ensuring members feels horny during the hectic day journey. With fun activities like late night video calls, company at social events, and our all members trust each other to deliver(bas kehne ki baat hai) , Team AURA members helps make college life memorable by balancing work(nam ka), fun(bakchodi), and personal(hamare yeha personal kuch nhi he) connection(gf hai nhi kisi ka).
